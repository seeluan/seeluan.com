About Me

About Me

I began my career as a graphics and web front-end designer, creating digital experiences with care, creativity, and attention to detail. I took a break from professional work to raise my children, but art has always been a part of my life. Even while stepping away from full-time work, I continued exploring my creativity and taught myself oil painting.

I started with tutorials from Bob Ross, but soon discovered my true passion in portrait oil painting, capturing the expressions, personalities, and emotions of families, children, and adults. I love bringing life and warmth to each piece, creating artworks that you and your loved ones can treasure for years.

Whether I'm painting or designing, I focus on combining skill with creativity and care, so every piece feels meaningful, personal, and lasting. My goal is to bring your story, personality, or vision to life, whether on canvas or screen.

Commissions

Commissioning a Portrait is Easy


Share a few of your favorite photos and tell me your vision for the piece, and I'll bring it to life on canvas. Once finished, your painting will be delivered straight to your door, ready to be cherished.

Portrait Fees

  • 9x12" or similar sizes - US$500    (limited to one subject only)
  • 11x14" or similar sizes - US$800
  • 16x20" or similar sizes - US$1,500
  • 18x24" or similar sizes - US$2,000
  • 24x30" or similar sizes - US$2,500

    • Prices are for one subject only. Each additional subject: +25%.

    A 50% deposit is required to begin work, with the remaining balance due before delivery. Each painting typically takes 1-3 months to complete. Frames and framing are not included. Please note that the deposit is non-refundable.

    If the commission involves travel, the customer is responsible for lodging and travel expenses.

    Payment: Cash or check preferred; PayPal accepted.

    Shipping: Not included; shipping costs vary per canvas at the time of delivery.


    With no obligation, please contact me for an estimate.
